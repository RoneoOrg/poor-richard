Spotlight PA combines the strengths of [The Philadelphia Inquirer](https://www.inquirer.com) in partnership with [PennLive/The Patriot-News](https://www.pennlive.com) and dozens of other news organizations across Pennsylvania in a historic commitment to investigative reporting on state government and beyond. Additional distribution partners allow us to share resources and maximize audience and reporting power. We provide our content **free of charge** to newsrooms across the state to help bolster local media and better inform the public.
